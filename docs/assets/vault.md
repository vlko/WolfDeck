# vault

Round-door treasury safe — the anchor prop of the finance pack.

## Look

- Slate box body (`slateGray`) on a `charcoal` plinth with a matching
  charcoal top lid.
- Round door: `stoneGray` ring with charcoal rivets around a dark
  (`charcoal`) opening; slate door disc with a big charcoal rosette
  handle (tapered hub, three crossed spokes, outer rim) and hinge blocks.
- With `open: true` the door swings out on its hinge and two `strawGold`
  coin stacks peek from the dark opening.
- Blob shadow.

## Dimensions & origin

≈ 1.65 wide × 1.9 tall × 1.45 deep (door adds ≈ 0.2 in front when closed).
Origin at ground-center.

## Options

| option | type | default | notes |
| ------ | ---- | ------- | ----- |
| `seed` | number | 0 | facet jitter |
| `open` | boolean | `false` | swings the door open, gold visible inside |

## Animations

`animations: none` — static asset.
