# schoolhouse

The village school — a two-storey landmark building for the schoolyard and
"city in miniature" scenes.

## Look

Cream box body with a parchment trim band between the storeys, dusty-rose
gabled roof with eaves overhang, front gable panel carrying a paper-white
clock face (`deepBrown` ring, charcoal hands at a seeded time). A small bell
gable sits astride the ridge: cream post, dark arch, `sunGold` bell with a
`deepBrown` clapper, dusty-rose cap. Double plank door (`plankBrown`) with
lintel and a stone step; two rows of paper-white framed windows, each pane
randomly lit or dark. Blob shadow.

## Dimensions & origin

≈ 4.6 wide × 4.3 tall (bell gable top) × 2.6 deep. Origin at ground-center.
