# moneyBag

Tied money sack with an embossed € sign.

## Look

Faceted `tanBrown` sack (squashed icosahedron) with a gathered neck, a
`deepBrown` tie band and a small cone of cloth above it. On the front an
embossed € built from a `deepBrown` C-arc and two crossbars. Blob shadow.

## Dimensions & origin

≈ 0.75 wide × 0.95 tall. Origin at ground-center.
