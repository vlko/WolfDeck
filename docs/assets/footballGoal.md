# footballGoal

Paper-white football goal — sports-ground dressing, pairs with `ball`.

## Look

Paper-white front frame (two posts + crossbar) and angled side struts; a
`cloudWhite` paper panel leans from the crossbar to the ground behind,
striped with thin cream lines to read as a net. Mouth faces +z. Blob
shadow.

## Dimensions & origin

≈ 2 wide × 1 tall × 0.6 deep. Origin at ground-center.
