# barn

The big teal farmhouse with the mauve roof from the reference sheet — the
largest building in the set; anchors the back row.

## Look

- Wide duck-egg blue body (`duckEggBlue`).
- Shallow mauve prism roof (`roseMauve`) with overhang.
- Big dark round window with frame on the front face.
- Tall plank door, slate chimney, blob shadow.

## Dimensions & origin

≈ 4.6 wide × 4.1 tall × 2.5 deep at defaults. Origin at ground-center.
